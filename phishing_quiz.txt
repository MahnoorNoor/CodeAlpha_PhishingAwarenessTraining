Phishing Awareness Quiz (3 questions)

Q1. An email asks you to reset your password and it has lots of spelling mistakes. Should you click the link?
Answer: B) No — it looks suspicious.

Q2. You hover your mouse over a link and the domain is bank-secure-login.com while the email says it’s from YourBank. Is this safe?
Answer: B) No — domain is not the official bank domain.

Q3. Should you enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your accounts?
Answer: C) Yes — always when available.
